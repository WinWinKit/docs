WinWinKit is a platform for referral and affiliate marketing in mobile apps.
It enables you to leverage word-of-mouth marketing and grow your app's user base.

## Three Key Features

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Affiliates" icon="crown" href="/features/affiliates">
    Partner with bloggers, influencers, and content creators to promote your app
    through a commission-based campaign. Customize user rewards to maximize
    sign-up incentives.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referrals" icon="object-intersect" href="/features/referrals">
    Launch a mutual rewards system where current users earn incentives for each
    person they successfully refer to your app, while new users also receive
    welcome benefits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Promos" icon="ticket-perforated" href="/features/promos">
    Attract new users by distributing promo codes. Perfect for sales, special
    promotions, and shopping events like Black Friday.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
