## Reward types

WinWinKit supports five rewards types that enable vast range of possibilities
for engaging existing and attracting new users.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Basic" href="/features/rewards/basic">
    Simple reward to grant access, feature, or content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credit" href="/features/rewards/credit">
    Virtual currency reward.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Offer Code" href="/features/rewards/offer-code">
    Discounted or trial subscription and in-app purchase offer on the App Store.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="RevenueCat Entitlement"
    href="/features/rewards/revenuecat-entitlement"
  >
    Reward an Entitlement directly in RevenueCat.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="RevenueCat Offering"
    href="/features/rewards/revenuecat-offering"
  >
    Reward a custom Offering directly in RevenueCat.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

If a reward type you need is not supported yet, we would love to know about it.
Please [contact us](mailto:support@winwinkit.com) with your request.
