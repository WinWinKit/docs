WinWinKit is a platform for referral and affiliate marketing in mobile apps.
It enables you to leverage word-of-mouth marketing and grow your app's user base.

## Three Key Features

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Affiliates" icon="crown" href="/affiliates/affiliates">
    Invite bloggers, influencers, or other content creators to promote your app
    for a commission. Configure the rewards for the invited users for better
    incentivization.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referrals" icon="object-intersect" href="/referrals/referrals">
    Invite existing users to invite their friends, family, colleagues, and
    followers to your app for a reward for them and the invited people.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Promos" icon="ticket-perforated" href="/promos/promos">
    Attract new users by distributing promo codes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
